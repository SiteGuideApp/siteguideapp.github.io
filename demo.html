Our History

Sunrise Café opened in 2015 to serve freshly roasted coffee and homemade pastries. Founders Emma and Lucas wanted a welcoming space for the community.

We’ve expanded to include vegan, gluten-free, and seasonal specialties. Our coffee beans are sourced sustainably and roasted locally.

Community events, live music, and workshops are hosted regularly. Our café promotes connection, comfort, and creativity.

About Us

Our baristas are trained in espresso extraction and latte art. Every cup is crafted with care.

Pastries baked fresh daily. Croissants, muffins, and specialty breads use high-quality ingredients.

Sustainability is key: compostable cups, local ingredients, and recycling programs.

Catering available for businesses and events: coffee service, pastries, and snacks.
